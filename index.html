How It Works

The Twitter Early Response System provides timely on-the-ground intel on potential disasters.

It works by isolating a specific term in the global Twitter stream and then analyzes the frequency of that term to determine if the chatter is typical or heightened. It will then advise you on steps to take using a standard color-coded warning system.
